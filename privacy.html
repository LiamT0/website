Privacy Policy

Effective May 15, 2026

1. Who we are

LockedN ("LockedN", "we", "us") operates the LockedN beta service at lockedn.org. Reach us at liamjt@lockedn.org.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign in with Apple or Google we receive your name and email from the provider. We do not receive your password.
  • Connected financial accounts. When you link a bank account through Plaid we receive account names, balances, transactions, and identity attributes returned by your institution through Plaid.
  • Service usage. Standard request logs (IP address, user agent, timestamps) needed to operate and secure the service.
  • Communications. Anything you send us by email or through a form.

3. How we use information

  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Generate wealth projections, spending insights, and card-lock guardrails based on your linked accounts.
  • Respond to support requests and announce changes to the beta.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.

4. Plaid

We use Plaid Inc. to securely link your financial accounts. By using LockedN you authorize us and Plaid to access your financial information from your financial institution. Plaid's handling of that information is described in the Plaid End User Privacy Policy. LockedN never sees your bank login credentials.

5. Sign-in providers

Sign in with Apple and Sign in with Google authenticate you against your existing account at Apple or Google. We only receive the basic profile fields they release (typically name and email). If you use Apple's "Hide My Email" feature, we will only receive the relay address Apple provides.

6. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information and we do not share it with advertisers. We share information only with:

  • Service providers acting on our behalf (hosting, authentication, Plaid, email delivery) under contractual confidentiality obligations.
  • Authorities when legally required, narrowly and only to the extent compelled.
  • A successor entity in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to this policy.

7. Retention

We keep your account information and connected-account data while your LockedN account is active. You can ask us to delete your account at any time by emailing liamjt@lockedn.org. We will delete or anonymize data within 30 days of a verified request, except where retention is required by law.

8. Security

We use transport-layer encryption for traffic, encrypted storage for credentials, and least-privilege access controls. No system is perfectly secure; we will notify affected users without undue delay if a breach of personal information occurs.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, port, or delete your personal information, and to object to certain processing. Email liamjt@lockedn.org to exercise these rights. You can also revoke a linked bank account from inside LockedN at any time.

10. Children

LockedN is not directed to children under 18 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

11. Changes

We will update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be announced by email or in-app notice before they take effect.
