LockedN uses Plaid to read your accounts, balances, and transactions so your projection is based on what is actually happening. LockedN never sees your bank password.

You need to sign in before linking a bank account.

  • Data accessed: account names, balances, transactions, identity (name, email).
  • Why: to project net worth, flag spending leaks, and arm card-lock rules.
  • How long: kept while your LockedN account is active; deleted on request or account closure.
